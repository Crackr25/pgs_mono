## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Homepage Featured Products
**Goal**: Show 8 hand-picked best sellers

**Setup**:
- Display Mode: Manual
- Select: Your 8 best-selling products
- Title: "Featured Products"
- Description: "Our most popular items"

### Scenario 2: New Arrivals Auto-Update
**Goal**: Always show latest 12 products

**Setup**:
- Display Mode: Automatic
- Number: 12 Products
- Title: "New Arrivals"
- Description: "Check out our latest additions"

### Scenario 3: Category Highlight
**Goal**: Show only electronics products

**Setup**:
- Display Mode: Manual
- Search: "electronics"
- Select: All electronics products
- Title: "Electronic Components"

---

## Tips & Tricks

1. **Quick Selection**: Use search to filter, then select all relevant products
2. **Update Anytime**: You can change from automatic to manual mode later
3. **Mix & Match**: Use manual on homepage, automatic on other pages
4. **Product Order**: In manual mode, products display in the order you selected them
5. **No Product Limit**: Manual mode has no limit - select as many as you want
6. **Product Updates**: Changes to product details automatically reflect in showcase
7. **Deleted Products**: Removed products are automatically filtered out
